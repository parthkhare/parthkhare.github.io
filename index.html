SociocaRtography R · Machine Learning · GIS

Parth Khare

I am currently working as Manager in Data Science divison at Nielsen and have worked extensively on parametric and non-parametric statistical algorithms on dense Insurance, Consumer, Public Census and Satellite Imagery data. I have conducted training sessions on R and Decision Trees. Recently, I innovated a model architecture in R, which estimates socio-economic attributes at 1 square km level precision GRID. . The algorithm, implemented on Asian and European geographies works by interacting public census data with micro satellite imagery data to unearth spatially intelligent patterns. The hidden potential of layering multiple data sources has driven me work on developing open source solution to aid public policy. Starting with some interactive map applications on Indian data: India Crime Analysis, Delhi Crime Deployment, R Shiny Maps & Voronoi Maps.

I have developed several customized geo-spatial functions in R to ease spatial geometry processing in R. In addition I have created an optimsiation function, GBM optimizor to help in choosing optimal parameters values(trees, shrinkage, OOB etc) for focused identifcation of local and global minima. Learning R for the first time can get involving due to multiple syntaxes for same operation, R Utility Kit is an attempt to streamline codes on basic data operation, classified by commonly used processes string manipulations, merging/reshaping etc.
