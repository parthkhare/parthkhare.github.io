The offlate brouhaha over Big Data and Pattern (read Data) Sciences distracts us from tracing it's genesis, emnating from our subconcious. Remember and try to think the common cord between the last book on Sherlock Holmes or the last movie on alien invasion or a National Geography piece of gladiatorial struggle between a snake and mongoose. For any of these pieces to have a climax, It is only after a string of high profile murders that Doyle's invulnerable suspect leaves a clue to help Holmes create a patern. It's only after the nerd protagonist has tried permutations of armheads on different parts of the alien exoskeleton that he/she finds a literal chink in the armor. Similarily, it takes the mongoose couple of bites before it accurately gauges speed and mobility of the snake. All these calculated assessment or patterns are not much different from the estimated probabilties of lab experiments.

A repeated event creates a pattern and a minute iterated observation of patterns is what inculcates learning. We have evolved ourselves by doing this subliminal process of observing, learning and taking actions. How else do we explain having from having a pagan god to a marked insurance for each natural catastrohy ? Not only unearthing patterns but even basic tools of infereing form them are not exactly a new thing as well. Many machine learning algorithms (neural network, decision trees) all broadly subscribe to basic principle of fine-tuned recursive learning.

So if perceptional Data Sciences has existed as a part of our psyche then what is the new data ? In dearth of a universal defintion of Big Data, on simple deconstrcution Big Data should simply more number of rows and columns. And that is precisely what is different about Data Sciences as being evolved today. I think it's only now that we can layer and condense multiple sources of information on single event, transaction or individual together. It's like having multiple perspectives on the same event only in the case of data they get harmoniously alligned to serve an objective.

The intent of this page is not unimaginatively different from the other blogs/io pages which I believe all subscribe of an overarching idea of creating infomration symmetry and reduce time for learning new skills and techniques and produce more works of scholarship benefitial to the society.
